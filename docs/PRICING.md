## Pricing Philosophy

**All agents are included in every plan.** Unlike competitors who gate AI features behind expensive tiers, Bevyly gives every customer access to the full autonomous sales workflow. Plans differ by volume and advanced features, not core capabilities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to pay extra for AI agents?
No. All agents are included in every plan. Plans differ by volume limits (leads, credits, minutes), not by which agents you can use.

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
You can continue using Bevyly. Overages are billed at the rates listed above. You'll receive alerts at 80% and 100% of your limits.

### Can I change plans mid-cycle?
Yes. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes. All new accounts get a 14-day free trial of the Max plan with no credit card required.

### What's included in the trial?
Full access to all features with Max limits (2,000 leads, 50K AI credits, 5 seats). No credit card required to start.

### Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?
Yes. Verified 501(c)(3) organizations receive 50% off any plan. Contact support with your determination letter.

### Can I add extra seats without upgrading?
Yes. Additional seats are available:
- Pro: +$49/seat/month
- Max: +$39/seat/month
- Ultra: +$29/seat/month

### What's your refund policy?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.
